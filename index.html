Capabilities

Everything your team needs to ship with certainty

From first commit to production, Lumen tracks the signals that separate teams that ship fast from teams that ship well.

Real-Time Quality Gates

Automated analysis scores every pull request against your team's standards. Catch regressions before they reach main — not after a 3am alert.

DORA Metrics, Finally Useful

Deployment frequency, lead time, and change failure rate — surfaced in context, trending over time, and tied back to the commits that moved them.

Deployment Health Dashboard

A single pane of glass for every service you ship. Know exactly what's in production, what's pending, and what needs your attention — before users notice.

Technical Debt Mapping

Stop guessing where your codebase is fragile. Lumen tracks complexity trends, hotspot files, and coverage gaps across every sprint — so planning refactors is a decision, not a gamble.

Security & Dependency Intelligence

Continuous scanning of your dependency graph against live vulnerability feeds. Zero-day awareness built into your workflow, not bolted on as an afterthought.

Team Velocity Insights

PR cycle time, review bottlenecks, and contributor patterns — presented as coaching data, not surveillance. Help your best engineers spend less time waiting and more time building.
